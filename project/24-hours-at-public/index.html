24 Hours, Public

24 Hours at the Public Hotel.

PUBLIC Hotel is the gritty, glamorous brainchild of Studio 54 legend Ian Schrager. Situated in the heart of the LES, PUBLIC feels like a temple to the neighborhood’s inimitable zeitgeist. Wild and artful, smart and playful, PUBLIC mirrors the rhythms of its city: buttoned down and caffeinated at sunrise, sweaty on the dance floor at sunset.
