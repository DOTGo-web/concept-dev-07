Who’s Oliver?

In the past, we might’ve imagined a Charles Dickens character or maybe some kind of mischievous cartoon animal. After working with the iconic eyewear brand Oliver Peoples, our record was set straight: Oliver is all of us. Or all “peoples,” one might say. Wordplay aside, we produced this film in collaboration with Oliver Peoples for their excellent Who’s Oliver campaign? The synergy between Oliver Peoples elegant realness and black and white aesthetic...and our own...resulted in a strong, short piece steeped in the hustle of NYC.
