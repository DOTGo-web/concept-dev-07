E-Tron Launch, Audi

Stephanie Gilmore is a six-time world champion surfer, a homebody for nature and an astute reader of the ocean’s moods. To no one’s surprise, she’s deeply passionate about environmental protection. 

Stephanie teamed up with Audi to celebrate the release of their first electric car the E-TRON. As you may have guessed, CONVICTS was in on the action. In between chatting about the surf scene and soul of San Francisco, we made this video with Steph and the Audi gang.
