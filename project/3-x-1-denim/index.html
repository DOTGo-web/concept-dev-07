3x1 Denim

3x1 Denim, Agency

3x1 Denim was founded by Scott Morrison, America’s foremost denim guru.

Their jeans define that rock and roll chicness of New York’s Lower East Side. They’re effortlessly sick pieces of denim that are democratic and upscale at the same time. Convicts A) loves eating pasta with Scott and B) was stoked to collaborate with his brand on these films about 3x1’s fall 2019 collection and their collaboration with Mimi Cuttrell. Hot denim and cool vibes abound.
